About Lanka Lens

A public account of what this is, how it works, what it costs, and where it's going.

Origin

Why this exists

We have enough of news and news outlets. What we don't have is a single, accessible, multilingual (TBA) digest without the noise or bias (TBA) of any one outlet.

Lanka Lens is my personal project built to learn and test out the latest and greatest tech available to us.

"I mainly follow a few Instagram pages owned by some news outlets. Google News is not optimized for regional granularity. Sometimes I'd read an article but not know the full context." — DatVicious, builder

The digest is free to use and will stay free as long as it's viable to run. I will ask for donations later.

Honesty

Known limitations

  • AI translation of Sinhala and Tamil is functional but may miss cultural nuance.
  • Some news sites block RSS fetching — Economy Next, Virakesari, and Hiru are not yet included.
  • The AI occasionally produces duplicate stories or misidentifies story relevance.
  • Stories are filtered to the last 36 hours. Breaking news after 7AM appears the following day.
  • All news sources carry editorial bias. Outlet bias labelling is a planned future feature.
