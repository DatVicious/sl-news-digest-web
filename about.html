About Lanka Lens

A public account of what this is, how it works, what it costs, and where it's going.

Origin

Why this exists

Sri Lanka has no shortage of news. What it lacks is a single, accessible, multilingual digest that pulls from Sinhala, Tamil, and English sources simultaneously — without the political noise of any single outlet.

Lanka Lens started as a personal project. I found myself spending too much time navigating multiple news sites, Instagram pages, and WhatsApp forwards every morning. This bot does that work automatically.

"I mainly follow a few Instagram pages owned by some news outlets. Google News is not optimised for regional granularity. Sometimes I'd read an article but not have the full context." — DatVicious, builder

The bot is free to use. It will stay free as long as it's viable to run.

Honesty

Known limitations

  • AI translation of Sinhala and Tamil is imperfect. Translations are functional but may miss nuance.
  • Some news sites block RSS fetching. Economy Next, Virakesari, and Hiru News are not yet included.
  • The AI occasionally produces duplicate stories or misidentifies story relevance.
  • Stories are filtered to the last 36 hours. Breaking news that occurs after the 7AM digest will not appear until the next day.
  • All news sources carry editorial bias. Lanka Lens does not currently rate or label outlet bias — this is a planned feature.
  • Cookie consent walls on some sites prevent full article fetching via Jina AI.
